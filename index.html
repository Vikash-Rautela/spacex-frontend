Rural Tourism

Vision

Main Features

Focus on nature and agriculture

Rural tourism often focuses on activities linked to nature and agriculture, such as farming, fishing, crafts, and various aspects of rural lifestyle.

Authentic experiences

Rural tourism can offer travelers authentic experiences of local culture and lifestyle. Travelers can stay in homestays, eat local food, and participate in traditional activities.

Sustainable tourism

Rural tourism is often seen as a more sustainable form of tourism than mass tourism. It can help to support local communities and reduce the environmental impact of tourism

Local experience

Homestays offer travelers a chance to experience the local culture and lifestyle firsthand. Travelers can interact with local people and learn about their customs and traditions.

Authentic accommodation

Homestays are often housed in traditional buildings and offer travelers a unique and authentic accommodation experience.

Support for local communities

Homestays can help to support local communities by providing income to local people and promoting the local culture.
